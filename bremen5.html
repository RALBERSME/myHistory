S.S. Bremen completed its journey from Bremerhaven via Southampton and Cherbourg to New York on July 16, 1929.
It covered the distance between Bishop Rocks and the Ambrose Lightship in 4 days, 18 hours and 17 minutes at an average speed of 27.83 knots, setting a new record and winning the Blue Riband, which has been awarded to the fastest Atlantic liner since 1838.
