What would YOU do?

Imagine you were the captain of the Bremen.
What would you do if you were on the ship in the middle of the Atlantic and you found out that your country had started a war and that the surrounding countries that you had to pass through with the ship had declared war on your country in return?

Each time you click on the ADD button, a new box is created for a new idea. Just type it into the box and press enter. Your input will be saved to localStorage. This way you will be able to compare your ideas with the captain plans on the next page.
