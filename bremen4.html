Look where I was built & launched: Bremerhaven on the North Sea / Germany

The Deutsche Schiff- und Maschinenbau started building me on June 18th, 1927. I was lauchned on August 16th, 1928, completed on July 5th, 1929 and started my maiden voyage on July 16th, 1929. My sister ship is the S.S. Europa.
